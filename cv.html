Career

Brendan has been a comedian for the last fifteen years and is in great demand on the after-dinner and corporate circuit throughout Great Britain. He has also performed in Germany, Portugal, Spain and regularly in The United States. He was the guest speaker at the Grand Order of the Water Rats annual Ball at the Grosvenor House 2003 and is himself now a Water Rat. He is a director of Newcastle Panto Ltd. and performs his solo show in theatres.
